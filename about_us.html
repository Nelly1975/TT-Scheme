Defence Forces Trainee Technician Scheme

- The purpose of the Corps of Engineers Trainee Technician Scheme is to provide military trades persons for the Corps. The scheme offers successful candidates the opportunity to become qualified and experienced Combat Engineers as well as the opportunity to pursue the trade for which he/ she is selected. The system of training is of the highest standard and will lead to a trade qualification accredited by SOLAS, the national trades training authority.

- An offer of a place on a COE TT Scheme is dependent on the candidate first successfully completing the Combat Engineer Course. Vacancies currently exist for Trainee Technicians for the following trades:
  • Electrician
  • Carpenter
  • Plumber
  • Construction Plant fitter
  • Refrigeration
  • Brick Layer
